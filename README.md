# quietnotes

Local-only meeting notetaker for Obsidian. Captures audio from your Mac, transcribes with `mlx-whisper`, summarizes with Ollama — entirely on your machine. Nothing leaves the laptop.

> **Status:** v0.1 in development. macOS-only (Apple Silicon, macOS 15+ required for unified system+mic capture via ScreenCaptureKit).

See [`docs/design-idea.md`](docs/design-idea.md) for the full design, scope, and roadmap.
